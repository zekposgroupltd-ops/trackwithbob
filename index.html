Our Services

WHAT WE OFFER

Professional, confidential, and legally compliant tracking and recovery support. Every case is handled with discretion and urgency.

📱

DEVICE RECOVERY ASSISTANCE

Helping victims of phone, laptop, and gadget theft to recover their property through coordinated and lawful means.

What It Is

We assist individuals whose phones, laptops, tablets, or other electronics have been stolen. Using tracking data, IMEI tracing guidance, and professional coordination, we help build a recovery pathway that works within legal boundaries.

How We Help

  • Advise on immediate steps after theft
  • Assist with IMEI reporting and carrier coordination
  • Coordinate with local law enforcement where applicable
  • Guide through telecom and platform escalation channels

Who It's For

  • Individuals with stolen smartphones or laptops
  • Small businesses with missing equipment
  • Anyone who has had gadgets taken by force or theft
🚗

VEHICLE TRACKING ASSISTANCE

Professional support for vehicle owners dealing with theft, unauthorized use, or location uncertainty.

What It Is

We assist vehicle owners in locating stolen or missing vehicles by helping them access existing GPS data, coordinating with tracking service providers, and supporting proper police reports.

How We Help

  • Help interpret vehicle tracker data
  • Coordinate with tracking device providers
  • Support documentation for police reports
  • Liaise with relevant transport authorities

Who It's For

  • Car owners dealing with vehicle theft
  • Fleet operators monitoring assets
  • Individuals verifying vehicle location
👤

MISSING PERSONS SUPPORT

Compassionate, professional support for families and individuals dealing with the distress of a missing loved one.

What It Is

We provide structured support and professional guidance to families seeking to locate missing persons — including elderly relatives, missing children, or individuals who have lost contact. All assistance is provided lawfully and sensitively.

How We Help

  • Guidance on filing official missing persons reports
  • Coordination with relevant authorities
  • Assistance with information gathering
  • Communication support throughout the process

Who It's For

  • Families of missing adults or elderly relatives
  • Parents concerned about missing children
  • Individuals who've lost contact with family
🛡️

FRAUD CASE SUPPORT

Helping victims of financial scams and online fraud document, report, and seek recovery through proper channels.

What It Is

We assist fraud victims in organizing their cases, documenting evidence, and understanding the proper reporting pathways — including relevant financial institutions, EFCC referrals, and cybercrime units.

How We Help

  • Help compile and organize case documentation
  • Advise on EFCC and cybercrime unit reporting
  • Coordinate with banks for transaction tracing
  • Provide referrals to legal professionals if needed

Who It's For

  • Victims of online and investment scams
  • Individuals defrauded via mobile money or transfers
  • Businesses dealing with internal financial fraud
